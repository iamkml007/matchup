📜 BGMI Squad Tournament Rules

Professional | Fair Play | Competitive Integrity

1. Basic Rules

  • Match mode: Squad (TPP/FPP - to be decided by admin)
  • Map: Erangel (or as announced by admin)
  • Team size: 4 players (no extra/sub without permission)
  • Room ID & Password: Will be provided 10 minutes before match

2. Entry & Payment Rules

  • Entry fee: ₹40 per squad
  • Payment in advance is compulsory
  • Fake booking = Direct Ban ❌
  • No refund policy after match confirmation

3. Player Eligibility (Important)

  • Minimum level: Level 40+
  • Minimum tier: Platinum or above
  • New ID / Fresh account = NOT ALLOWED ❌
  • All players must have same team name/tag

4. Anti-Hack Rules (Strict)

  • ESP / Aimbot / Wall hack = Permanent Ban
  • iPad view / Config hack / GFX tool abuse = NOT ALLOWED
  • Emulator players = NOT ALLOWED (Mobile-only tournament)
  • Teaming (2 squads playing together) = Disqualification

5. Proof Rules

  • Every team's squad leader must keep screen recording ON
  • Kill proof / winner proof screenshot is compulsory
  • Proof not submitted = Kill count will NOT be considered ❌

6. Kill & Points Rules

  • Per kill reward: ₹2 / ₹3 (as decided by admin)
  • Finish kill only counts (knock does NOT count)
  • Self kill / grenade abuse will NOT be counted

7. Winner Decision Priority

  1. Chicken Dinner 🥇
  2. Total kills
  3. Survival time

8. Timing Rules

  • Late entry = NOT ALLOWED
  • Cannot join room after match starts ❌
  • No remake (except server-wide issue)

9. Disqualification Rules

  • Wrong IGN / player change without permission = DQ
  • Hack use = Permanent Ban
  • Abuse / Gaali = Removal from tournament
  • Admin decision is final (no argument)

10. Prize Distribution

  • Payment within 10–30 minutes after match
  • Payment via: UPI / Paytm / as decided by admin
  • Fake claim = Permanent Ban

⚠️ Admin Decision is Final | No Refund Policy ⚠️

By participating, all players agree to abide by these rules. Any violation will result in immediate disqualification and permanent ban from future tournaments.
